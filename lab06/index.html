Taylor Swift

You Need to Calm Down

"You Need to Calm Down" is a song recorded by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on June 14, 2019, as the second single from her seventh studio album, Lover (2019).

As the fourteenth track on the album, Swift wrote and produced the song with Joel Little, who also co-wrote and co-produced her previous single "Me!". "You Need to Calm Down" is an upbeat, summery synth-pop and electropop song, with a chorus of ascending echoes. It sees Swift addressing internet trolls and homophobes, voicing her support for the LGBTQ+ community.
